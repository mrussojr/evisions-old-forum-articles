Are you a Banner® by Ellucian customer faced with the impending move from Banner 8 to Banner 9? Would you like to alleviate as many obstacles – and headaches – as possible that could arise from that migration? Well, have we got good news for you! Evisions Argos can help you with your transition to Banner 9. Here’s how:

Validation

As you transition from Banner 8 to Banner 9, you will need to validate that the data is moving through the forms correctly. Traditionally, the validation process is very manual and extremely time consuming. Argos, though, can set up that process so that, on one screen, you can instantly compare data from Banner 8 with that from Banner 9.

If you’ve had Banner for a number of years, chances are you changed business rules somewhere along the way. This often results in ‘messy’ data. Argos will help present the data in a way that makes the data disconnects easier to see, and provide audit reports that show where problems may lie.

Oracle Forms Customizations

Migrating to Banner 9 will require a lot of time and attention from your IT staff – especially for those institutions who have custom forms built in Oracle Forms. These forms will become harder to customize as Banner 9 moves closer to the cloud. However, Argos can replicate these forms. Evisions Professional Services experts can move the customizations in these forms into Argos. Your IT staff, then, can focus on training users in Banner 9.

IT Staff Augmentation

Transitioning to Banner 9 is a huge project. It can tie up a significant portion of your IT resources, aside from the work in Oracle Forms. For instance, if your IT department is also tasked with producing reports for your institution, that production could wane as their time and focus turns to Banner 9.

With Argos, your enterprise reporting can be put into the hands of the end users. There is no lengthy installation and it’s easy to modify as your reporting needs change. End users can access the reports they need when they need them, without having to submit requests to IT. And should you have any complex reporting projects, our Professional Services team can assist you every step of the way. This will allow your IT staff to give the move to Banner 9 the focus it deserves.

To conclude, the combination of Argos functionality and offerings from Evisions Professional Services can save you a lot of time and frustration – not to mention those headaches! We offer peace of mind to the IT departments, and the institutions themselves, undergoing the migration to Banner 9.
