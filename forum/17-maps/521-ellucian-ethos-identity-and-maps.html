Hello,

 

PortalGuard currently doesn't support the deep linking (directly to the report) at this time. I have an open ticket for this because of the anchor tag (#) will be cut off on the request (ReturnUrl). They are trying to resolve this so deep linking can work. Yet you will have to modify the link to always use service parameter with CAS or PortalGuard. Even CAS by default has this issue which is due to URL Fragmentation (solution: https://apereo.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CASUM/pages/103261327/Preserving+URL+anchor+fragments+on+login+form+submission).

 

PortalGuard CAS 

Example:

https://argos/Argos/awv/#explorer/<ReportPath>/<SubPath>/<Report>

 

Becomes:

https://<yourIDP>/cas/login?service=https://argos/Argos/awv/#explorer/<ReportPath>/<SubPath>/<Report>
