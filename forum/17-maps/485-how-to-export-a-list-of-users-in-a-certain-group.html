I know this is an old post, but for anyone looking.  You need to join the SysUser, SysGroup, and User_Group tables to get that information.  Here's what we use:

 

select SysGroup.Name,
       SysUser.Name
  from SysUser inner join ( SysGroup inner join User_Group on SysGroup.Id = User_Group.Group_Id ) on SysUser.Id = User_Group.User_Id
