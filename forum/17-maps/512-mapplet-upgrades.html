On our Argos server, we intentionally removed FormFusion, Intellicheck and DataMasque.  Since we are not using those applications on that particular server, there is no reason to keep them.   We use those other applications on separate servers.

 

Note that certain versions of MAPS may necessitate upgrading the other applications.  Review the Product Version Matrix to avoid compatibility issues.
