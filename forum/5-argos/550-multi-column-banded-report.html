 Hi Ehall!

 

 I'm in full agreement that the multi-column options are terrible.  We've been trying to create a room roster, but due to the way the bands generate, we get a lot of phantom floating residents without room headers in the beginning of the second band.

 

  However, given your situation: Are you just trying to do a "This Year vs. Last Year" comparison between sets of categories?  It might be possible to do it with 2 separate data sets.

 

  For example, you could have a Majors report datablock that pulls 2 separate term codes, and have the report query generate a list of majors that were present in at least one of those two terms.  Inside the banded report itself, you could create a dataset - FirstTermMajors that pulls an aggregated count of majors for that term code, and a second dataset that pulls an aggregated count for the second term, then link each of the datasets to the Argos data set on MAJR_CODE.  In the report itself, you can put the counts from each data set on the same band, and add a divider to separate the data between years.

 

  If this sounds like something that could work for you, and you'd like me to generate an actual datablock (and report) showing what I mean, let me know, and I can put something together!
