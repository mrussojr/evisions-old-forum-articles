I don't have a datablock readily available to send, but I just pull the Repeat Course Ind from SHRTCKN. It might vary at different institutions, but my experience has been that an indicator of "I" flags the course for inclusion and an "E" for exclusion. If the indicator is null there is no repeat and for those that have a repeat, we pull the grade from the "I" record. I hope this helps.
