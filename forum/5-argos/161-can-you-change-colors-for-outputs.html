On a Banded Report, yes.  For example, I have a banded report that shows datablocks in our Testing folder.  Datablocks older than a certain date are eligible for deletion.  Even though the deletion date appears on the report, I like to highlight it in red.  In my case, I have a report query column called "CAN_DELETE".  The deletion date field in the banded report is named "DB_Deletion_Date"  I then add an expression field and set the expression to this:  SETFONTCOLOR('DB_Deletion_Date', IF((ArgosData.CAN_DELETE = 0), RGBCOLOR(0, 0, 0), RGBCOLOR(255, 0, 0)))

 

Is that what you're looking for?
