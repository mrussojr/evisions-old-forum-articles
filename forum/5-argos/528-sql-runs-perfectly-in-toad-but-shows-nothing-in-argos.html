I have a small datablock that runs perfectly in TOAD, but does not produce anything in Argos.  Has anyone else encountered that headache?  The SQL looks fine.  I can't figure why I am not getting any output in Argos.

 

Here is my code:

 

-- **************************************************************************************************
/*
Report:     All detail codes and their corresponding FOAP Report
Author:     Krishna Kancherla
Create:     07-02-2017
Purposs:    The report is to list all detail codes and their corresponding FOAP.
Comments:   Requested by CE Steven Sparks

*/
-- **************************************************************************************************
select distinct a.ssrfees_term_code     term_code,
                a.ssrfees_crn           crn,
                c.ssbsect_subj_code     subj_code,
                c.ssbsect_crse_numb     course,
                c.SSBSECT_SSTS_CODE     status,
                a.ssrfees_detl_code     detail_code,
                a.ssrfees_amount        amount,
                a.ssrfees_user_id       user_id,
                a.ssrfees_activity_date activity_date,
                b.tbracct_b_fund_code   fund,
                b.tbracct_b_orgn_code   orgn,
                b.tbracct_b_acct_code   acct,
                b.tbracct_b_prog_code   prog,
                b.tbracct_b_actv_code   actv
  from ssrfees a
  join tbracct b on a.ssrfees_detl_code = b.tbracct_detail_code
  join ssbsect c on a.ssrfees_crn = c.ssbsect_crn
                    and a.ssrfees_term_code = c.ssbsect_term_code
                    and a.ssrfees_term_code = :parm_term

  JOIN (SELECT D.SSRATTR_ATTR_CODE CAR_INST,
               D.SSRATTR_TERM_CODE,
               D.SSRATTR_CRN
          FROM SSRATTR D
         WHERE
            D.SSRATTR_ATTR_CODE   IN ('CEAC','CEBU','CEHB','CEMT','CEPS','CEST','COAV',
                                         'COCO','COKC','COLT','COPS','COSW','COCS')
       ) CINST
                 ON a.ssrfees_term_code = CINST.SSRATTR_TERM_CODE
                 AND a.ssrfees_crn      = CINST.SSRATTR_CRN


 where :parm_BT_Run is not null
   and c.SSBSECT_SSTS_CODE = :PARM_LB_STATUS
