I believe Terry is correct.  The subqueries are for conditions on the main query.  One recurring example is a subquery to get the maximum sgbstdn_term_code_eff that is less than or equal to the selected term in a listbox on a form and then that sgbstdn_term_code_eff is used in the condition of the main query to get the right sgbstdn record.  Yes, that can be done with a f_get_sgbstdn call, but I see a lot of people do it with a subquery.
