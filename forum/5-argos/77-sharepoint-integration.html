We have two forms of SharePoint integration with Argos. We've configured a document library to accept emails (Library settings > Incoming email settings ) and then we have a report scheduled to email to this library.  We also use custom SharePoint lists with links to the generated APIs of a lot of our report reports - but this was before the eLauncher was available.
