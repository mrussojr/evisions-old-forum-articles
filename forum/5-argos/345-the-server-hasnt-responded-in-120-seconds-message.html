Hello Ed 

 

Depending on you how patient your end users will determine timeout setting. I have seen clients go  from 30 seconds to 5 minutes, but most clients I have seen set it at 120 seconds.
 

Below are a couple of links to KBs  that discuss the timeouts in Argos/MAPS. In regards to the Argos Web Viewer,  currently there is no dialog box "Cancel or Continue" after the timeout limit has been reached it will just cancel. Our Engineering team is looking into a resolution to this,  but at this time I do not have a time frame as to if/when this feature will be added.
