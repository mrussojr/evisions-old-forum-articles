Issue:  Meeting WCAG 2.0 AA Requirements in Argos Dashboards and Reports

 

We are attempting to meet WCAG 2.0 AA Requirements with all our applications and products.

Evisions has some ADA Features in the Argos Web Viewer, but not the Argos Client.

 

As noted in Evisions Blog, "Improving Accessibility for Argos Dashboards", by Alwyn Durham (Oct 19, 2017), 

there are some things you can do in your Dashboard to improve the accessibility: 

https://evisions.com/improving-accessibility-argos-dashboards/#comment-1531 

 

  • Labeled By
  • Tab Order / Tab Stop
  • Button and Link Text
  • Color, Contrast, Font and Size

 

When evaluating Dashboards (using aXe), many requirements are not met.

I've opened several Support Tickets to request enhancements to meet the following WCAG 2.0 AA Requirements:

  • Zooming and scaling
  • HTML Language Attribute
  • ARIA Parent Roles
  • Comment on eLauncher page regarding AWV containing ADA Features, but not Argos Client

 

 

  1. Is your institution struggling to meet WCAG 2.0 AA requirements in your Dashboards and Banded Reports?
  2. What ADA Compliance issues are you having?
  3. Have you requested any enhancements to meet WCAG 2.0 AA Requirements?

 

WCAG 2.0 AA Requirements are a hot issue.  When evaluating products for purchase, it is a top concern.

 

Thanks,

Mike

Jon,

     Using an ADO connection to the Argos/Maps data warehouse (well documented by Evisions in case you don't have a connection like this yet.) We have a query that looks for datablocks where there is no datablock note with a title of 'ADA', which is what I title the notes. So, a piece of it would be like below. 

Select <whatever datablock info you want> where the Datablock.ID is not in(

select DataBlockNote.DataBlock_Id
           from DataBlockNote
          where DataBlockNote.Title ='ADA')

 

Does this help?
