Following what Ace said, there are a handful of ways to do this in the datablock. What I have moved toward doing for datablocks that exist to send out emails is that I have a multicolumn box on the form that shows a good preview of results, and then augment the report query with a lot of columns to ease the creation and distribution of the emails. Sometimes these are concatenated fields that provide a burst condition when a pidm or something simple won't do. If I am sending a banded report pdf attachment, I do a bunch of concatenation columns so the layout will be somewhat consistent.
