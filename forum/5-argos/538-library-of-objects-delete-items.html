The owner does have the option to delete items from the library, but it is in a place you would not expect.  It is in the Tools option of the main menu.  Click on Tools and you will then see Library of Objects.  Click on Library of Objects and you will then see your various objects in your library.  Click on the object you wish to delete and then click on the red "x" and you should be all set.

 

I hope this helps...
