Do you have access to the Banner documentation, in particular the entity relationship diagram?  That may help  when joining tables.

 

At any rate, I don't have access to that Banner form, but based on the tables, here is my guess.  The SORLCUR_KEY_SEQNO field has this comment: 

KEY SEQNO:  Sequence number of key record, saradap_appl_no, srbrecr_seqno, shrdgmr_seqno, sgrstsp_key_seqno.

That makes me think the join would look like this: 

SELECT 
    SARADAP_APPL_NO,
    SARADAP_TERM_CODE_ENTRY,
    SARADAP_APPL_DATE,
    SARADAP_ADMT_CODE,
    SORLCUR.* 
FROM 
    SARADAP
    JOIN SORLCUR ON (SARADAP_PIDM = SORLCUR_PIDM AND SARADAP_APPL_NO = SORLCUR_KEY_SEQNO)  

HTH

David, You may also need to check the SORLCUR_LMOD_CODE. Not sure if this is standard across all Banners or if it's individualized by institution, but our SORLCUR uses 4 LMOD_CODEs depending on the source of the info -- RECRUIT (srbrecr), ADMISSIONS (saradap), LEARNER (sgbstdn), OUTCOME (shrdgmr). So, if you're pulling data from SARADAP, you'd want to be sure the LMOD_CODE='ADMSSIONS' (assuming your Banner uses the same LMOD_CODEs).  And you may also want to check that the SORLCUR_CACT_CODE='ACTIVE'. 

 

I generally just look for the max seq_no for the LMOD I'm interested in. I don't think I have an example using SARADAP, but for active students I use this to figure out which SORLCUR/SORLFOS info DegreeWorks is likely to extract:

 

FROM SPRIDEN
JOIN SORLCUR ON SORLCUR.SORLCUR_PIDM = SPRIDEN.SPRIDEN_PIDM
             AND SORLCUR.SORLCUR_CACT_CODE = 'ACTIVE'
             AND SORLCUR.SORLCUR_LMOD_CODE = 'LEARNER'
             AND SORLCUR.SORLCUR_SEQNO =
                        ( SELECT MAX(getmax.SORLCUR_SEQNO)
                          FROM SORLCUR getmax
                          WHERE getmax.SORLCUR_PIDM = SORLCUR.SORLCUR_PIDM
                            AND getmax.SORLCUR_PRIORITY_NO = SORLCUR.SORLCUR_PRIORITY_NO
                            AND getmax.SORLCUR_CACT_CODE = 'ACTIVE'
                            AND getmax.SORLCUR_LMOD_CODE = 'LEARNER'
                        )
LEFT OUTER JOIN SORLFOS ON SORLFOS.SORLFOS_PIDM = SORLCUR.SORLCUR_PIDM
                        AND SORLFOS.SORLFOS_LCUR_SEQNO = SORLCUR.SORLCUR_SEQNO
                        AND SORLFOS.SORLFOS_CSTS_CODE = 'INPROGRESS'
                        AND SORLFOS.SORLFOS_CACT_CODE = 'ACTIVE'
