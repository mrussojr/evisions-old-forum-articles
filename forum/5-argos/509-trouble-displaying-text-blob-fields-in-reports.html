I believe Argos had issues in the past dealing with Blobs.  

We got around rendering them by doing substrings:

 

SELECT SUBSTR(BLOB_NAME,1,4000) || SUBSTR(BLOB_NAME,4001,4000)

 

We don't have any MySQL running to by knowledge, but it looks like you could do something like

 

SELECT CONCAT(SUBSTRING(BLOB_NAME,1,4000), SUBSTRING(BLOB_NAME,4001,4000))

 

Please let me know if you get anywhere with it!
