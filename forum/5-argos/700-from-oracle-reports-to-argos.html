Hello:

 

We created groups for our users and then use MAPS to limit what they are able to access in ARGOS based on the Groups Assigned to each Folder.   In your case, each datablock/report could be assigned to specific groups.  If a user is not assigned to the specific group, then that datablock/report would not be visible in there session.

 

I think that would be the easiest. 

 

For us, all of our reports used across campus are create through batch PL/SQL processes and then users access them through a report repository, like ePrint.

 

Within ARGOS, only or AD-Hoc report users are able to access report and we limit those based on group memberships.

 

Hope this helps.
