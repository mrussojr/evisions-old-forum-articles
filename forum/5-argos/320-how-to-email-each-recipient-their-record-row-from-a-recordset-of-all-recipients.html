Your Edit Schedule should look like this:

        Execute the report

        Bursting

                 Process and save

                 Send an Email

 

Notice the indenting of the 'Process and save' and 'Send an Email'.   This will result in your example about will net 138 separate emails.   To force the indent just have 'Bursting' highlighted then add the 'Process and save' and 'Send an Email'.  

 

Hope this helps.
