When you are in the visual design mode, you can use the copy at the top of the form and use the paste to move to the new location or form.  That will keep it in Visual design mode.  You can also copy and paste any item on a form to another form or even copy and paste an entire form.   If you already have the datablocks built, it might be easier to copy your existing forms(one at a time) and paste each form to your new datablock and set the 'Is Tab' property to 'Yes' for each.

 

Good luck,

John
