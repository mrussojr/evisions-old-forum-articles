Hello,

I am having trouble creating a datablock containing multiple OR and AND operators which is simple enough in free sql.

I would like to do the following in Visual Designer:

 

select CHARGECREDIT.PEOPLE_ORG_ID,
       CHARGECREDIT.AMOUNT,
       CHARGECREDIT.CHARGE_CREDIT_CODE
  from CHARGECREDIT CHARGECREDIT
 where CHARGECREDIT.PEOPLE_ORG_ID ='XXXXXXXX' AND
       (CHARGECREDIT.CHARGE_CREDIT_CODE ='PAYCASHCHK' OR
       CHARGECREDIT.CHARGE_CREDIT_CODE ='PAYCREDCRD') AND
       YEAR(ENTRY_DATE) = '2018'

 

Thank you,

Hi Hylon,

 

For this, you want to use conditional groups.  The PEOPLE_ORG_ID  and ENTRY_DATE checks can go in as normal.  Then, create a new and conditional group and add the two CHARGE_CREDIT_CODE options.  For this particular query, you can use the IN operator.  E.g.: 

where
	CHARGECREDIT.PEOPLE_ORG_ID ='XXXXXXXX' 
	AND CHARGECREDIT.CHARGE_CREDIT_CODE IN('PAYCASHCHK', 'PAYCREDCRD')
	AND YEAR(ENTRY_DATE) = 2018

I also find it helpful to click the View SQL button to compare what ARGOS generates to what I expect.

 

HTH
