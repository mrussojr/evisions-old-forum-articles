In your ORDER BY  clause, you could do something like: 

SELECT STVTERM_CODE FROM STVTERM
ORDER BY 
    CASE WHEN STVTERM_CODE LIKE ('8%') THEN STVTERM_CODE END ASC,
    CASE WHEN STVTERM_CODE NOT LIKE ('8%') THEN STVTERM_CODE END ASC
