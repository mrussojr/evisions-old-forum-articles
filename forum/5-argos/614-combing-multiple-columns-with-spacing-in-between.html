I am building a datablock and want to add a "Printed Name" to the report but it combine multiple columns with spacing such as:

 SPRIDEN_FIRST_NAME || " " || SPRIDEN_MI || " " || SPRIDEN_LAST_NAME AS "Printed_Name",

I am getting this error:

 

(EOCINativeException) [FireDAC][Phys][Ora] ORA-00904: " ": invalid identifier

 

Is there a way to add a space between each field?

try:

 

SPRIDEN_FIRST_NAME || ' ' || SPRIDEN_MI || ' ' || SPRIDEN_LAST_NAME AS Printed_Name

 

don't put quotes around actual field names and use single quotes for the spaces
