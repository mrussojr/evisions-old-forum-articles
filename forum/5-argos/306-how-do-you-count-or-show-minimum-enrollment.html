Sorry, this one is hard.  There may be a written policy for your institution that states a minimum enrollment for faculty to receive full pay and a prorated pay schedule.  This could vary by level and can be overridden to allow teaching out for retired programs etc.

 

The issue is complicated by crosslisted sections - different CRNs but really the same course/instructor/time etc.  Perhaps some of the CRNs are ITV.

 

But there really isn't a minimum enrollment field in Banner.
