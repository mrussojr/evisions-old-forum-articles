On the datablock in form design, one of the properties is "Is Tab."  By setting this to Yes, the form will be displayed as a tab.  The "Tab Name" property controls the name of the tab.   Here are two links which may be helpful:

  • http://webhelp.evisions.com/HelpFiles/Argos/en/Default.htm#DataBlocks - Form Design - Properties.htm%3FTocPath%3DDataBlocks%7CForm%2520Design%7C_____1
  • http://webhelp.evisions.com/HelpFiles/Argos/en/Default.htm#DataBlock Designer Guide/Using Multiple Forms in a.htm

Yes, I believe that is correct; you cannot change the font of the tab name.  One option you could do instead is to set the Is Tab property back to "No" and use buttons to switch between forms.  Then, set the On Click of the button to activate each form.  But, you'd have to replicate the button on each form.  HTH.
