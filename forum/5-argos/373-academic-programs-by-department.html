I use this query to get the major and department connection:

 

select distinct
               bb.DeptCode,aa.sorcmjr_majr_code "MajorCode"


from    saturn.sorcmjr aa inner join
              (select sorcmjr_dept_code "DeptCode",max(sorcmjr_term_code_eff)"MaxEffTerm"
               from saturn.sorcmjr 
               where sorcmjr_dept_code is not null and sorcmjr_dept_code<>'0000'
               group by sorcmjr_dept_code)bb
             on aa.sorcmjr_dept_code=bb.DeptCode


order by 1,2

 

Our major codes can belong to more than one college and dept codes can belong to more that one major... it just depends on the student really.  If you want to tie them all together you could use sgbstdn (get the max of sgbstdn_term_code_eff to get the  most current record) -- it could be different from student to student.  You'll have to dig into your data to find that, but sgbstdn is a good place to start.
