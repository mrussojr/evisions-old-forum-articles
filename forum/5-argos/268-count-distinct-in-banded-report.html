Here is how you could do it in Oracle as a SQL query:

  

select Class_Year,
	   count(distinct donor_pidm) Donors,
	   sum(Dollar_amount) Dollars
from example_table
group by Class_Year

Try to use row_number function in your report query.

 

ex.

select class_year, 

              donor_pidm,

              dollar_amount,

             decode(ROW_NUMBER() OVER(PARTITION BY class_year, donor_pidm order by donor_pidm),1,1,0) AS donor_count

from example_table

 

Then on your report subtotal, you can do a SUM on the donor_count column.
