We are creating a banded report that will list student names and ids in a header, followed by the courses they take in a detail band.  Some students take 1 course, others take 3 courses and others take a few more.  We want to place a rich text field immediately after the last detail record for a student, but do not know which kind of band to place after the last detail band, so that we end up with a "floating" end of form.

 

Can someone please tell us what band should be used, and should it be linked to any other band?  We usually create CSV files and seldom use banded reports, which i why we are asking.

One way you could do it would be to make a group header and group footer.  The student name would go in the group header and your rich text field would go in the group footer.  When the student name changes, the group footer will print creating your 'floating' end of form.  Hope this helps.
