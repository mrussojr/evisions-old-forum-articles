David,

That would be a decision made by the class scheduling staff on where to store what code.  The 2 Banner schools I have worked for have stored the online code various places. Check the validation tables to see if a relevant entry is listed.  Here are some likely places to check:

  • SSBSECT_SESS_CODE (VALIDATION TABLE IS STVSESS)
  • SSBSECT_INSM_CODE (VALIDATION TABLE IS GTVINSM)
  • SSBSECT_CAMP_CODE (VALIDATION TABLE IS STVCAMP - they used online as a specific campus)
  • SSRMEET_ROOM_CODE OR SSRMEET_BLDG_CODE

 

Another clue could be that SSRMEET DAYS and TIMES are missing

 

hope that helps.
