Hi Sajjan,

 

You can query it from your MAPSDATAWAREHOUSE connection. You can change this around depending on your folder structure, but something like :


select *
from datablock
where path like '(somepath).%'

 

-- can also use --

 

-- where folder_ID = (whatever your folder_ID is)

 

-- or whatever you are looking for

 

I went through this a few months back when trying to decide what folders to move into PROD for one of the departments that had been using TEST as PROD. Sam Cook provided me with: 

 

select *
from datablock
where path like 'Student.%'

 

Saved me a lot of time, not only figuring out what they had, but also figuring out what they weren't using.

 

Good luck!

Cale
