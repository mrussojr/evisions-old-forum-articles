Dave,

 

I have done this before.  As an example, say you are creating a report for a College of their students.   The detail record is the actual student id, name, department, major, etc. data.

 

The outer group band would be for department and would be set to force a page break when the department changes.

The next inner group band would be based on Major and would be set to force a page break on change of major

In the middle would be your detail band.

 

For this to work properly, you will need to add a sort to your query (or report) to sort by Department, Major, Student Infor (ID, Last Name, etc).

 

Since the sorted data will be grouped, it will allow for each of the group bands to operate properly.

 

I hope this helps.

 

Terry
