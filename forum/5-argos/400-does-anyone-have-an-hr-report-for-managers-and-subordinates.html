David,

 

I don't know if our school can help, because it depends on how you store your data in Banner. Does your organization use the nbrjobs_supervisor_pidm, _supervisor_posn, and supervisor_suff fields? If so, you can query those and join to spriden, nbbjob, nbrposn, etc. to get the most up-to-date informtion. If your place is anything like our, you'll need to ensure you account for the max effective_date for the corresponding TERMED job record (we pre-date our job termination records, especially for part-time faculty).
