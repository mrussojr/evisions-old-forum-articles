Hi Ian,

 

Here is an example of the order tags within a free type report query:

  

WHERE
	(s.name = :LB_Status OR '(All)' = :LB_Status)
	AND (rt.ShortName = :MC_RequestTypes.ShortName OR '(All)' = :MC_RequestTypes.ShortName)
	AND (r.CreatedAt >= :DE_Submit_Start OR :DE_Submit_Start IS NULL)
	AND (r.CreatedAt <= :DE_Submit_End + 1 OR :DE_Submit_END IS NULL)
	AND (r.Completed >= :DE_Completed_Start OR :DE_Completed_Start IS NULL)
	AND (r.Completed <= :DE_Completed_End + 1 OR :DE_Completed_end IS NULL)
--$addfilter
--$beginorder
ORDER  BY rt.Name, r.completed DESC
--$endorder

In essence, you have to add the  --$beginorder and --$endorder tags.  Note, when you click the Insert Special button, the in-product help gives great examples for each type of tag.
