Recently, we have been working on exception reports and a desire was identified to have summarizing emails with record counts or other information in the body (not the actual report output). When referencing the ArgosData object for the body text of the email, it appears there is no row enumeration- only the first record is yielded. Of course we could take this into an API route to accomplish the goal; however, we would like to avoid the additional time required to develop the solution.

 

Our current solution is to concatenate the summarizing information to be returned with ASCII 13 delimiters into a single record. The handler that produces and sends the email properly interprets the carriage returns and we have effective multi-record output in the email body this way. The problem is that the concatenations occur in free form SQL, so maintenance and changes to the exception datablocks require more SQL-able persons.

 

Does anyone know of another way to get row enumeration to occur in the body of a scheduled task email output?
