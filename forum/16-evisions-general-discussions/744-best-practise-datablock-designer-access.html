Vernonique,

 

We use AD to assign group access to our users.  Most users have report viewing access and some folders have specific department and/or division security depending on the needs of the users.  Most of our users do not wish to have datablock access or create reports.  This function happens in the IT department.  IT produces the reports and functional users and/or IT run and validate the report data and then moves them into production.

 

Hope this is helpful,
