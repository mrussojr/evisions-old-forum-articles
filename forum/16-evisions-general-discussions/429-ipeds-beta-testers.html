Evisions is seeking 3-5 institutions who would like to participate in our BETA testing program for a solution that assists institutions with IPEDS surveys.  With the support of BETA testers, we’re able to elevate our solutions to a higher quality level before releasing them to the market.

 

As a participant, you can share your ideas and together we can create the best solution possible for assisting with IPEDS Surveys.  When you join the Beta Testing Program, you’ll enjoy the following benefits.

·        Getting a look at a new solution before anyone else

·        The satisfaction of providing your suggestion

·        Improve the solution as a result from testing

·        Receive a discounted purchase price, which is given to active BETA testing participants

 

How to participate

·        Your college or university is based in the United States

·        You and your college or university complete IPEDS surveys

·        Your ERP is Banner® by Ellucian

 

To learn more, and to be considered for the IPEDS BETA Testing program email your contact information to don.ashley@evisions.com
