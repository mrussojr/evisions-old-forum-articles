Evisions is looking for a qualified consultant who is a subject matter expert (SME) with experience coordinating, preparing, and submitting IPEDS surveys for a four-year higher education institution.  The consultant must have a strong working knowledge, and understanding of, Ellucian Banner ERP.  The consultant will provide guidance for Evisions to understand how to compile information for IPEDS surveys from student information systems and various external sources.      

 

Major Responsibilities:
 

·        Provide guidance for Evisions staff to understand how to compile information for IPEDS surveys from student information systems and various external sources  
 

·        Contribute to Evisions understanding of the various typical processes for the preparation and submission of IPEDS surveys
 

·        Assist in the preparation, creation, and validation of baseline SQL queries to source IPEDS related information from Ellucian Banner ERP tables
 

·        Provide guidance on IPEDS survey validations
 

·        Share, and document commonly made errors by Institutions during the coordination, preparation, and submission of IPEDS surveys
 

·        Document best practices for completing IPEDS surveys
 

·        Develop, create, and deliver high-level IPEDS informational sessions to Evisions teams (i.e., Engineering, Product Management, Professional Services, and Product Support)

 

The consultant can work remotely or onsite along with the Evisions team in Irvine, CA.  If this opportunity interests you please feel free to contact me directly.
