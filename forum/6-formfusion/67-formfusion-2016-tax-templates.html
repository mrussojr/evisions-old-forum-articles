*** IMPORTANT UPDATE 2/2/2017 ***

The Evisions FormFusion 1042-S template currently on our website is compatible with pxr1042.pc version 8.13.1 only. 

Ellucian just alerted us that they will be releasing a new version of pxr1042.pc (version 8.13.1.8) scheduled February 24, 2017 to correct an issue with the print values in Boxes 4a and 4b .  This new version will impact the FormFusion template mapping, so Evisions will be releasing a new 2016 FormFusion 1042-S template that will be compatible with pxr1042.pc version 8.13.1.8.  We are planning to release the new 1042-S FormFusion template shortly after Ellucian releases the HR 8.13.1.8 patch scheduled for February 24, 2017.

For details on the Ellucian defect and changes included in the Ellucian HR patch 8.13.1.8, please contact Ellucian and refer to defect # CR-000145775.
